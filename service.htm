Community Involvement

Adult Basic Education

Minneapolis, MN

Adult Basic Education (ABE) is a program run through Minneapolis Public Schools, which has English Language Learning classes (ELL) in reading, writing, and math. They also have high school diploma programs, citizenship classes, and GED preparation and testing,to name a few.

I worked with Adult Basic Education in Minneapolis for a year while serving as an AmeriCorps VISTA member. I assisted in computer literacy by helping students, who were primarily recent immigrants, to create an email account and use English language learning software.

Minnesota Coalition for the Homeless

Minneapolis, MN

The Minnesota Coalition for the Homeless works on behalf of homeless people in Minnesota. They lobby for rights and interests of homeless people and to inform and educate people about the homelessness

I volunteered with the Minnesota Coalition for the Homeless during my time serving as an AmeriCorps VISTA. I worked on training materials for them in order to educate people on what homelessness in Minnesota looks like.

World Vision

Vienna, Austria

World Vision is an international non-profit that creates sponsorship programs with communities in a number of nations outside the US. The sponsorships provide education, food and even buildings in the communities. Sponsors can also visit the children they sponsor and send letters and cards.

I volunteered with World Vision while I lived and worked in Vienna, Austria. I worked in the office filing papers, entering data, and translating letters into English that went to sponsored children.

AmeriCorps Service

Minneapolis Public Schools

Minneapolis, MN

I served as a VISTA in Minneapolis Public Schools as a part of Volunteer MPS, a department which focuses on involving volunteers in the schools. There was a total of 15 VISTAs that served in different sites in the district from the Volunteer MPS department. I served with the Homeless and Highly Mobile Student Services Department.

I helped with donations at the shelter office, created attendance reports and surveyed parents about after school programming. As part of the department I helped to spread the information about the McKinney-Vento law that provides rights for homeless students across the country.

City Year Chicago

Chicago, IL

I served as a Corps member with City Year Chicago at the Dulles School of Excellence on the southside of Chicago.I served as part of a team that worked on improving school climate and student attendance, homework, and behavior.I served in an eighth grade English classroom, where I supported students in learning grammar, literary devices, and reading skills.

I served as the literacy coordinator and as one of the after school coordinators. I worked to encourage reading in the school and created after school programming for sixth through eighth graders.
